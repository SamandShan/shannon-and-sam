A love story

How It All Began

It started with a shared shift at The Chart House restaurant in Melbourne — Shannon behind the host stand, Sam clearing tables as a busser. Trained together as servers, something clicked from the very first day.

"Friends for 10 years. Together for 5. Partners for life."

What started as a friendship built on late-night shifts and a shared love of the Florida coast slowly became something more. Through the years, they became each other's person — the one you call first, the one you want beside you on every adventure.

Five years ago, friendship became love. And on one golden sunrise at their beach, it became forever.

She Said Yes

Sam surprised Shannon with what she thought was a sunrise surf session at "their beach" — only to walk out and find him on one knee, surrounded by roses planted in the sand, champagne, and the perfect song playing.
